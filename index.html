Who We Are

CyberSisco is an MSME-verified education startup (Government of India) dedicated to strengthening the digital world through cybersecurity and digital forensics education, research, and practice. Guided by the principle "Protect | Detect | Secure", we aim to empower students, professionals, and cyber enthusiasts with practical knowledge and real-world skills to combat cybercrime in the digital era.

With a passionate team of cybersecurity learners, researchers, and practitioners—supported by experienced mentors and advisors—CyberSisco is committed to nurturing the next generation of cyber professionals and promoting excellence in cybersecurity and digital forensics across India and beyond.

We focus on building a strong and collaborative cyber community by promoting hands-on training, research innovation, and professional development. Through workshops, conferences, webinars, research projects, and student engagement programs, CyberSisco acts as a bridge between academia, industry, and law-enforcement practices.

Our Identity

1

Education Startup

An Education Startup is an organization that focuses on innovative learning, skill development, training, and research using modern methods, technology, and practical approaches. Unlike traditional institutes, education startups aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world industry requirements.

2

Community Focused

A community-focused organization is one that places people, collaboration, and shared growth at the center of its mission. Instead of working in isolation, it builds a strong, inclusive ecosystem where learners, professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts come together to learn, share, and grow collectively.

3

Research Driven

A research-driven organization is one that builds its work on systematic study, evidence-based practices, and continuous innovation. Instead of relying only on existing knowledge, it actively explores new ideas, analyzes real-world problems, and develops practical solutions. At CyberSisco, being research-driven means learning through investigation, experimentation, and real-world analysis to strengthen cybersecurity and digital forensics practices.
